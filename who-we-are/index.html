Whoweare

dRaX ARTS launched as a Creative Agency in 2010, by the founder of WOO Interactive, the Premier Mult- imedia and Digital Design Company in Nigeria...

And quickly began forming rewarding part- nerships with clients of all sizes, from small local businesses through to large national brands – many of which retain our services

Over the years, processes and procedures have changed quite a bit, and we have changed with it

We now have better resources and in-house expertise, and even more exceptional talent, all bringing their unique perspectives and ideas to the work table.

However, we hold the same philosophy today as we did back then – to help clients meet their business objectives using our ever-increasing knowledge and deep passion.

Even when the techniques and tools require a unique approach, our focus remain fixed on blending creativity with effective ideas to help our clients achieve their business goals.

But what we're most proud of is the long-lasting relationship we have with our clients.

We have become, somewhat, an extension of their marketing teams due to our open and honest approach and an ability to produce fresh ideas that cut through the clutter.

It has helped each of those organizations build their brand, communicate effectively with consumers and achieve their commercial targets.

We are a team of friendly, creative and hard-working people who share a passion for the unique.

It is this that gets us out of bed in the morning, (well, this, coffee and our cozy little glass studio) and more often than not, late to bed at night.
